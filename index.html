How We Work

Our Design Process

A structured, collaborative path that takes your project from preliminary concepts to final execution support.

01

Consultation

Initial introductory call or meet-up to map out broad ideas and design goals.

02

Requirement Discussion

Defining detailed functional requirements, budget targets, and aesthetic tastes.

03

Site Visit

Physical walkthrough of the project space to check structural orientations.

04

Space Measurement

Detailed dimensional surveys of partitions, heights, columns, and plumbing points.

05

Design Concept

Visual mood boards, fluted layouts, 2D blueprints, and initial space distributions.

06

Material & Finish Selection

Picking acrylic sheets, veneers, quartz countertops, hardware fittings, and palettes.

07

Final Design Approval

Consolidating all 3D renders, itemized costs, schedule metrics, and signing off.

08

Execution / Construction Support

On-site monitoring, vendor alignment, electrical point supervision, and assembly oversight.
